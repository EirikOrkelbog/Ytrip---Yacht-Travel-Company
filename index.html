Benefit of yacht travel

  • Best views

    • The opportunity to see much more than a hotel resort. Each day the yacht sails to new places, discovering new landscapes and beauties for you.

  • Port cities

    • Free time in port cities. You can request for stops in ports of your choice, were you can see museums, gallerys or other attractions.

  • Silence

    • No ambiguous crowds of tourists. When on vacation the noise from the hotel, city and beach often bothers. Vacation on a yacht is designed to escape crowded places.

  • Diving and snorkeling

    • The widest opportunities for diving and snorkling. The routes pass through lagoons with magnificent coral reefs, tunnels and caves avalible for scuba diving and snorkling.
